How It Works

Emergency Request

Recipients can send urgent blood requests with priority levels (Critical, High, Medium, Low) and specific deadlines.

Smart Matching

Our system automatically finds compatible donors based on blood type, location, and availability status.

Instant Alerts

Compatible donors receive immediate notifications with priority indicators and emergency details.

Auto-Escalation

If no response within 30 minutes, the system automatically escalates to all donors in the region.
